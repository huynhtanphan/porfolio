Personal Finance Analysis
- This personal finance project empowers me to take control of your spending. By tracking daily expenses, analyzing trends, and visualizing data, you can identify areas to save and make informed financial decisions to reach your goals.
Goal:
- Track daily personal expenses
- Analyze spending trends over time
- Understand spending habits and make informed financial decisions
Methodology:
- Record daily expenses in the Notion app
- Group expenses into categories: living expenses, monthly spending, shopping, fuel, other
- Connect the Notion database to chartbase.so
- Create charts of expenses by category, time, and months
Data Analysis:
- Identify large expenditures
- Find potential areas to cut back on spending
- Compare spending to previous months
- Create a budget for the future
Project Dashboard:
- Click here for more details: notion.so/phanhuynhtan/Persoanl_Finance_Analysis
Benefits:
- Control spending effectively
- Save money
- Achieve financial goals
- Gain a better understanding of spending habits
Tools:
- Note-taking app
- Notion database for expense management
- Chartbase.so for data visualization
Data Used:
- Track daily personal expenses
- Data Type: name of expense, amount, category, date
Notes:
- Data is collected from March 20, 2023 to the present
- This project can be customized to fit the individual needs of each person
